Oficina Da Capoeira - Bristol

Our group aim at teaching the essence & art of Capoeira, its philosophy and respect. It is an enriching mix of music, dance, martial arts and Afro-Brazilian culture. Some people say Capoeira is a dance with an element of fight, some say that it is a fight with an element of dance. ”Capoeira e tudo o que a boca come!” – Mestre Pastinha
